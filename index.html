SEO Deep-Dive

My client wanted to identify profitable opportunities to target improved SEO rankings. I analysed rank, search volume, and competitiveness of their search terms, prioritised by profitability for the company, and identified substantial niche areas for the client to focus their SEO strategy on. I used Python to clean my data and build the database, and Tableau to create relevant visuals to aid explanations to non-technical and non-marketing stakeholders.

Eurovision Trends (in progress)

I started nerding-out over the history and influences of Eurovision results and wanted to see if I could build some cool visualisations of trends or even predictions of what factors contribute to placing in the top 5 overall positions. I was particularly interested in the relationship (if any) between the professional jury vs general population scores. I am using SQL and Python for data management and querying and Tableau to build the visual story.

Ferry Operator Costs Analysis (in progress)

A ferry operator client wanted to optimise the costs of regular maintenance and servicing against the improved fuel efficiency gains. This project involved detailed analysis of operational data, predictive modeling to forecast fuel consumption, and cost-benefit analysis to determine optimal maintenance schedules. I am building this dashboard as a web app using Dash in order to reduce reliance on new tools for this very small company.
