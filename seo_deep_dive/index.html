Project 1: SEO Deep-Dive

My client wanted to identify profitable opportunities to target improved SEO rankings. I analysed rank, search volume and competitiveness of search terms, prioritised by profitability for the company, and identified substantial niche areas for the client to focus their SEO strategy on. I used Python to clean my data and build the database, and Tableau to build custom, relevant visuals to aid in explanations to non-technical and non-marketing stakeholders.
